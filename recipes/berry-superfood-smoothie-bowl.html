Berry Superfood Smoothie Bowl

Link to recipe source

An easy smoothie bowl recipe that makes a breakfast packed with antioxidants. Plus, its vegan and gluten-free.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mixed frozen berries
  • 4 ice cubes
  • Splashes of light coconut milk to help properly blend ingredients
  • 1 tablespoon almond butter
  • Handful of spinach
  • Optional: teaspoon acai powder
  • Optional: maple syrup or sweetener of your choice
  • Optional Toppings: hemp seeds, coconut flakes, blueberries, or freeze dried strawberries

Steps

  1. Place the frozen berries, ice cubes, coconut milk, almond butter, and spinach in a blender. Add acai powder if using. Blend until smooth, adding coconut milk as necessary. Taste and add maple syrup or sweetener of choice. Blend again if necessary.
  2. Pour into two bowls and top with hemp seeds, coconut flakes, blueberries, and dried strawberries if desired.
